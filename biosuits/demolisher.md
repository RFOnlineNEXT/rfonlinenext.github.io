## Details

 **Info: A biosuit equipped with devastating heavy weaponry, <br>embodying the peak of firepower combat.**

 **Description:** Excelling at mid- to long-range combat, it delivers devastating physical attacks and provides powerful fire support across the battlefield. <br>Drawing destructive energy at the heart of the battlefield, it unleashes overwhelming firepower to completely annihilate all enemies.

## Overview

Demolishers utilize heavy weaponry to deal consistent physical damage. Unlike traditional single-target marksmen, the Demolisher relies on **area-of-effect (AoE)** attacks and a unique **Overhit** mechanic to deal massive spike damage. While not as agile as the Phantom, the Demolisher makes up for it with raw destruction and the ability to clear large packs of enemies efficiently.

- **Role:** AoE Physical Attacker
- **Weapon:** Blaster Cannon
- **Attack Type:** Medium-to-Long Range / Physical
- **Special Mechanic:** Overhit (Low-probability, high-damage bursts separate from Critical Hits)

Introduced in the May 2026 update

## Skills & Mechanics

The Demolisher's kit revolves around explosive ordnance and shield generation to maintain uptime in fights.

| Skill Name | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Explosive Shell** | Core Buff | Converts basic attacks into **AoE attacks** for a limited duration. (Note: Damage to other players is reduced in PvP). |
| **Overhit** | Passive | Grants a low chance to trigger "Overhit" on attacks, dealing massive bonus damage independently of the Critical Hit stat. |
| **Reactive Armor** | Utility | Generates a shield that **blocks status abnormalities** (stuns, freezes), allowing the Demolisher to ignore CC and deal damage uninterrupted. |
| **Seas Blast / Shoulder Burst** | Active AoE | High-damage area-of-effect skills designed for clearing large mobs or clustered players. |
| **Impact Condenser** | Stat Buff | Increases **Parry Penetration**, allowing the Demolisher to ignore enemy parry mechanics for consistent damage. |

### Stat Priority

To maximize the Demolisher's potential, focus on the following stats in your skill tree and gear:

1. **Accuracy:** Essential to land high-powered hits.
2. **Overhit Rate:** To increase the proc chance of the unique burst mechanic.
3. **Physical Attack:** The Demolisher does NOT scale with Force Attack.
4. **AoE/CRT:** To amplify wave-clearing speed.

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Clear Speed" Farmer (PvE)

- **Focus:** Overhit Rate & AoE Damage.
- **Playstyle:** Activate **Explosive Shell** -> Group mobs -> Use **Seas Blast**.
- **Tip:** Invest in Physical Attack and Movement Speed to rotate between packs faster.

### 2. The "Siege Breaker" (GvG / PvP)

- **Focus:** Parry Penetration & Status Resistance.
- **Playstyle:** Pop **Reactive Armor** before entering a choke point -> Target the enemy backline.
- **Tip:** Stay close to your tank (Enforcer/Dreadnought). You are an area-denial weapon; do not chase kills alone.

## Verdict

The **Demolisher** is a must-have for players who focus on **PvE progression** and **large-scale faction wars**. While it lacks the mobility for top-tier 1v1 PvP, its unmatched AoE physical damage and unique Overhit mechanic make it one of the most satisfying classes for farming and siege warfare.
