## Overview

Phantoms are close-range physical attackers who rely on high mobility and evasion to survive. Unlike the sustained AoE of the Demolisher or the ranged debuffs of the Punisher, the Phantom focuses on **rapid single-target elimination**. The class features a unique HP absorption skill and evasion-boosting passives, making it highly durable in 1v1 scenarios despite its squishy appearance.

- **Role:** Single-Target Burst Assassin / Duelist
- **Weapon:** Dual Blades
- **Attack Type:** Melee / Physical
- **Special Mechanic:** HP Absorption (Circus/Sacrtus skill)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate to High (requires good positioning and target selection)

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Duelist" (Small-Scale PvP / Arena)
- **Focus:** Physical Critical Rate, Physical Critical Damage, Evasion.
- **Skill Priority:** Max **Finale** (for Airborne CC), **Will Penetrate** (for Stun), and **Insight/Blade Temper** (buffs).
- **Playstyle:** Open with **Shadow Assault** to gap-close -> Pop **Insight + Blade Temper** -> Use **Finale** to launch -> Follow up with **Storm Blade** or **Sonic Blade** -> Finish with **Deadly Blade**.
- **Tip:** Use **Circus/Sacrtus** when your HP drops to sustain through trades.

### 2. The "Backline Diver" (Large-Scale GvG / Quarry War)
- **Focus:** Guard Penetration, Movement Speed, Cooldown Reduction.
- **Skill Priority:** Max **Shadow Assault** (lower cooldown), **Will Penetrate** (for stunning key targets).
- **Playstyle:** Flank around the main fight -> Identify the enemy Technician or Punisher -> **Shadow Assault** in -> Stun with **Will Penetrate** -> Burst down -> Use **Phantom Dancer** to disengage or finish.
- **Tip:** Save **Spin Blade** for escaping if you get focused; the Bind effect can slow pursuers.

### 3. The "Evasion Tank" (Solo / Hybrid PvE)
- **Focus:** Evasion, HP, HP Drain.
- **Skill Priority:** Max **Evade Support** passive, **Circus/Sacrtus** (for healing), **Storm Blade** (low-cooldown filler).
- **Playstyle:** Sustain through PvE content by stacking evasion and using Circus to heal. Not optimal for farming speed, but viable for questing.
- **Tip:** Use **Blade Temper** for extra damage on elite mobs.

## Verdict

The **Phantom** is the quintessential PvP assassin in *RF Online Next*. While it struggles in PvE farming compared to ranged alternatives, its exceptional 1v1 burst, gap-closing mobility, and evasion-based survivability make it a top-tier choice for players who enjoy dueling and small-scale PvP.

If you are willing to invest time into learning its mechanics and don't mind suboptimal farming speed, the Phantom offers one of the most rewarding and skill-expressive playstyles in the game. However, beginners or players focused primarily on PvE progression may want to start with a more forgiving class like Punisher or Arbiter before transitioning to Phantom for endgame PvP content .
