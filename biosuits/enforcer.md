## Overview

Enforcers are close-quarters Force attackers who thrive at the front of any battle. Their kit includes a **taunt** to draw enemy aggression away from allies, a **stun** to lock down priority targets, a **barrier** for damage mitigation, and **self-healing** capabilities that make them difficult to kill. 

The Enforcer is described as "the shield of allies" and boasts the highest defensive performance among all biosuits, making it the go-to choice for players who want to lead the charge in dungeons and large-scale PvP.

- **Role:** Frontline Tank / Crowd Control
- **Weapon:** Force Sword + Shield
- **Attack Type:** Melee / Force Attack
- **Special Mechanics:** Taunt, Stun, Barrier Generation, Self-Healing
- **Difficulty:** Low to Moderate

### Stat Priority

To maximize the Enforcer's potential, focus on the following stats in your skill tree and gear:

1.  **Guard Rate:** Increases chance to block incoming attacks, triggering Reactive Guard.
2.  **Max HP / Defense:** Primary survival stats for tanking.
3.  **Status Abnormality Resistance:** Reduces duration of stuns and CC in PvP.
4.  **Force Attack:** Increases damage output for threat generation.

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Party Tank" (PvE Dungeons)
- **Focus:** Divine Shroud, Blessed Force, Guard Support, Max HP.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Divine Shroud** (barrier), **Blessed Force** (heal + cleanse), **Absolute Guard** (guard rate).
- **Playstyle:** Lead the party -> Use **Shield Charge** to engage -> Pop **Absolute Guard** and **Divine Shroud** -> Use **Shield Blow** (with Taunt add-on) to hold aggro -> Use **Blessed Force** to heal and cleanse debuffs.
- **Tip:** Coordinate with your Technician. Your job is to keep aggro and survive, not to top damage charts.

### 2. The "PvP Initiator" (GvG / Large-Scale)
- **Focus:** Shield Blow, Conviction Shield, Protection, Status Resistance.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Shield Blow** (stun + Accuracy debuff add-ons), **Conviction Shield** (stun), **Protection** (status resistance).
- **Playstyle:** Identify enemy backline (Punishers, Technicians) -> **Shield Charge** in -> **Shield Blow** to stun -> Follow up with **Conviction Shield** for extended CC -> Let your DPS clean up.
- **Tip:** Stack Status Abnormality Resistance to avoid being CC'd yourself. The **Protection** buff is essential before engaging.

### 3. The "Solo Farmer" (F2P Solo)
- **Focus:** Force Eruption, Blessed Force, Reactive Guard.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Force Eruption** (damage), **Blessed Force** (self-heal), **Reactive Guard** (survival proc).
- **Playstyle:** Group mobs -> Use **Force Eruption** for damage -> Use **Blessed Force** to heal and AoE -> Rely on **Divine Shroud** and **Reactive Guard** for defense.
- **Tip:** This build is slower than dedicated DPS classes but very safe. Perfect for AFK farming or dangerous zones.

## Verdict

The **Enforcer** is the definitive tank class of *RF Online Next*. It boasts the highest defensive performance among all biosuits, with an impressive toolkit that includes barriers, stuns, taunts, self-healing, and debuff cleansing.

For free-to-play and low-spending players, the Enforcer is a top-tier choice due to its low gear dependency and high demand in party content. If you enjoy being the frontline protector, initiating fights, and keeping your allies alive, the Enforcer is your suit.

Currently considered **one of the strongest classes** in the game based on Korean server feedback, the Enforcer excels in both PvE dungeons and large-scale PvP wars. However, if your goal is to top damage charts or farm mobs at maximum speed, you may want to consider a more damage-focused class like Punisher or Cypher for your main, while keeping an Enforcer alt for party content.
