## Overview

Technicians are ranged Force attackers who utilize drones and turrets to support allies and control the battlefield. Their kit includes healing drones, defensive towers that taunt enemies, and various debuffs that can be customized via skill add-ons. While their solo damage output may be lower than dedicated DPS classes, their exceptional sustainability and party utility make them invaluable in group content .

The Technician is frequently described as a "noble position" or "essential healer" in the community, with high party invitation rates .

- **Role:** Ranged Support / Healer / Debuffer
- **Weapon:** Force Gun
- **Attack Type:** Medium-to-Long Range / Force Attack (Magical)
- **Special Mechanics:** Healing Drones, Defensive Towers, Debuff Customization
- **Difficulty:** Low to Moderate

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Pure Healer" (PvE Party Support)
- **Focus:** Support Drone, HP Boost, Mind Recovery, Healing Rate.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Support Drone** (Ally Healing + Debuff Removal add-ons), **HP Boost**, **Mind Recovery**.
- **Playstyle:** Stay in the backline -> Maintain **HP Boost** and **Mind Recovery** buffs -> Use **Support Drone** to heal allies -> Use **Link Bullet** with ally healing add-on for additional heals.
- **Tip:** Coordinate with your tank. Your job is to keep everyone alive, not to deal damage.

### 2. The "Debuff Controller" (PvP / Dungeon Control)
- **Focus:** Satellite Cannon, Drone Assault, Defensive Protocol.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Satellite Cannon** (slow/freeze add-ons), **Drone Assault** (knockback add-on), **Defensive Protocol** passive.
- **Playstyle:** Position behind frontline -> Use **Satellite Cannon** to slow/freeze enemy clusters -> Use **Drone Assault** to knock back threats -> Use **Guard Tower** to taunt dangerous mobs.
- **Tip:** Customize your debuffs based on enemy type. Movement speed debuffs are great for melees; attack speed debuffs work well against rangers .

### 3. The "Solo Farmer" (F2P Solo)
- **Focus:** Guard Tower, Defensive Protocol, Battle Booster.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Guard Tower** (taunt), **Defensive Protocol**, **Battle Booster** (attack speed).
- **Playstyle:** Deploy **Guard Tower** to taunt mobs -> Pop **Battle Booster** for attack speed -> Use **Enhanced Shot** and **Battle Drone** to clear -> Self-heal with **Support Drone** as needed.
- **Tip:** This build is slower than dedicated DPS, but extremely safe. Perfect for AFK farming or dangerous zones .

## Verdict

The **Technician** is the essential support class of *RF Online Next*. While it may not top damage charts, its unmatched healing capabilities, debuff versatility, and party utility make it an invaluable asset in any group composition .

For free-to-play and low-spending players, the Technician is a top-tier choice due to its low gear dependency and high demand in party content . If you enjoy playing healer or support roles, want guaranteed party invites, and value sustainability over raw damage, the Technician is your suit.

However, if your goal is to solo farm quickly or dominate PvP leaderboards, you may want to consider a more damage-focused class like Punisher or Cypher for your main, while keeping a Technician alt for party content .
