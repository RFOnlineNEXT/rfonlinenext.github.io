## Overview

Dreadnoughts are close-quarters Force Attackers who thrive in the heart of battle. Their unique defensive mechanics allow them to significantly reduce damage coming from the front, making them natural counters to ranged classes. While their PvE farming speed is respectable, their true value shines in large-scale wars and guild raids where survivability is paramount .

- **Role:** Frontline Tank / Melee Attacker
- **Weapon:** War Axe
- **Attack Type:** Melee / Force Attack
- **Special Mechanic:** Front-facing damage reduction
- **Difficulty:** Low to Moderate

## Skills & Mechanics

The Dreadnought's kit revolves around gap-closing, area damage, and defensive cooldowns. Below are the core Active and Passive Skills based on in-game data .

### Active Skills

| Skill Name | Type / FP Cost | Cooldown | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Full Swing** | Active | - | Slam the axe down hard to deal damage to a single target. Core filler skill . |
| **Blow** | Active / Dash | - | Dash toward the target and swing the axe forcefully to deal damage. **Primary gap-closer** . |
| **Magnetic Ax** | Active | - | Throw a homing axe at a target to deal damage. Useful for pulling enemies or attacking from range . |
| **Double Crash** | AoE | - | Perform a spinning attack to deal damage to surrounding enemies. Core AoE farming skill . |
| **Mega Shield** | Defensive Buff | - | Activate a shield generator to **reduce damage coming from the front** and gain **immunity to stiffening/knockback** for a duration. Core tanking skill . |
| **Limit Release** | Offensive Buff | - | Release the biosuit's combat limiters, dealing damage to nearby enemies, **removing all debuffs**, and increasing **Physical Attack** and **Movement Speed** for a duration. Excellent for burst windows and escaping CC . |
| **Deadly Ax** | Finisher | - | Finish a "Finish Blow" state enemy by splitting their head with the axe. Grants a **Rage buff** and provides **damage and status abnormality immunity** during the animation . |

### Passive Skills

| Passive Name | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Capacity Engine** | Conditional | When you use a skill 3 times, **Attack Speed** and **Casting Speed** increase. Top priority passive for Dreadnought . |
| **Endurance** | Enhancement | Increases Critical Resistance and Critical Hit Damage Resistance . |
| **Tenacity** | Enhancement | Increases Status Abnormality Resistance . |
| **Axe Mastery** | Always Active | Increases Physical Attack Power . |
| **Life Support** | Always Active | Increases Max HP . |
| **Combat Guiding** | Always Active | Increases chance to ignore shield blocking and deals PVP fixed damage . |
| **Resistance Armor** | Always Active | Increases Status Abnormality Resistance and HP Natural Recovery . |

### Stat Priority

To maximize the Dreadnought's potential, focus on the following stats in your skill tree and gear:

1.  **Attack Speed / Casting Speed:** Critical for activating Capacity Engine and increasing overall damage output .
2.  **Defense Penetration:** Essential for dealing meaningful damage to armored targets in PvP .
3.  **Movement Speed:** Helps with positioning and gap-closing .
4.  **Max HP / Defense:** Primary survival stats for tanking.

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Immortal Farmer" (PvE Solo)
- **Focus:** Capacity Engine, Attack Speed, Double Crash.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Capacity Engine** first, then **Double Crash** for AoE clear .
- **Playstyle:** Pull groups of mobs -> Stack Capacity Engine (3 skills) -> Spam **Double Crash** -> Use **Limit Release** on elite mobs or bosses.
- **Tip:** Invest in HP recovery and defense passives to minimize potion usage during offline farming .

### 2. The "Frontline Wall" (GvG / Large-Scale PvP)
- **Focus:** Mega Shield, Limit Release, Defense Penetration.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Mega Shield** (frontal damage reduction), **Limit Release** (debuff cleanse), and **Combat Guiding** (PVP damage).
- **Playstyle:** Lead the charge for your guild -> Activate **Mega Shield** before engaging -> Use **Blow** to close gaps -> Pop **Limit Release** to cleanse enemy CC -> Disrupt enemy backline.
- **Tip:** Coordinate with Punishers or Cyphers behind you. Your job is to absorb damage and create space, not to secure kills.

### 3. The "Hybrid Bruiser" (Balanced)
- **Focus:** Life Support (HP), Axe Mastery (Attack), Capacity Engine.
- **Playstyle:** A balanced build for players who want to perform well in both PvE and PvP without respeccing.
- **Tip:** Prioritize stats in this order: Attack Speed > Defense Penetration > Max HP > Movement Speed.

## Verdict

The **Dreadnought** is the definitive frontline tank of *RF Online Next*. While it may not top the damage charts, its unmatched survivability, debuff cleansing, and frontal damage reduction make it an invaluable asset in any party composition. It is a forgiving class for beginners due to its high durability, and scales well into endgame content for players who enjoy controlling the battlefield .

If you want to be the unkillable wall that leads the charge in guild wars and laughs in the face of enemy rangers, the Dreadnought is your suit. However, if your goal is to top the PvP kill leaderboard, you may want to consider a more damage-focused class like Phantom or Cypher .
