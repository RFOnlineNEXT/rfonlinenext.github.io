## Overview

Psyphers are magical ranged attackers who harness Force energy to obliterate enemies from a distance. Their kit includes **taunts** (on monsters), **movement speed debuffs**, and powerful **AoE nukes**. The class also features a unique defensive passive that can grant brief invincibility when hit, making them surprisingly durable for a caster . 

Psypher is one of the few classes that performs well in **all content types** from the very beginning, making it an excellent choice for new players and veterans alike .

- **Role:** Ranged Magical Attacker / Crowd Control
- **Weapon:** Force Gauntlet
- **Attack Type:** Long Range / Force Attack (Magical)
- **Special Mechanics:** Taunt (PvE), Debuffs, Emergency Invincibility
- **Difficulty:** Low to Moderate

## Skills & Mechanics

The Psypher's kit revolves around controlling enemies with debuffs and taunts, then unleashing devastating Force explosions. Below are the core Active and Passive Skills based on in-game data .

### Active Skills

| Skill Name | Type / FP Cost | Cooldown | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Force Spear** | Active (Basic) | - | Throws a powerful spear made of Force energy at a single target. Core filler skill. Acquired via quest . |
| **Force Fist** | Active / Debuff | - | Drops a fist of Force energy on a target, dealing damage and **reducing their Movement Speed**. Excellent for kiting . |
| **Anima's Fragment** | Active / Taunt | - | Summons an Anima fragment near the target, dealing damage. If the target is a **normal monster**, it provokes them into attacking you. Unique caster taunt for PvE control . |
| **Unleashed Force** | Self-Buff | - | Releases Force energy, increasing **Force Critical Hit Rate** and **Force Critical Damage** for a duration. Core DPS buff . |
| **Force Cyclone** | Active / AoE | - | Creates a storm of Force energy at a target location, dealing **area-of-effect damage** over time. Essential for farming and mob clearing . |
| **Force Explosion** | Active / Burst | - | Throws enhanced Force spears to deal massive damage to a single target. Core bossing and PvP burst skill . |
| **Deadly Spear Finish** | Finisher | - | Finishes an enemy in a "Finish Blow" state by throwing multiple Force spears. Grants a **Rage buff** and provides **damage and status abnormality immunity** during the animation . |

### Buff & Utility Skills

| Skill Name | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Concentration** | Passive Buff | Increases **Force Accuracy**. Acquired from Skill Merchant . |
| **Awakening** | Passive Buff | Increases **Skill Damage** and reduces **incoming Skill Damage**. Acquired from Bellato Contribution Shop . |

### Passive Skills

| Passive Name | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emergency Armor** | Conditional | When the suit is hit, it accumulates energy and converts it into defense. **When hit, has a chance to become invincible for a short duration.** Excellent survival tool for a caster . |
| **Force Gauntlet Mastery** | Always Active | Increases **Force Attack** permanently. Acquired from Skill Merchant . |
| **Force Skill Support** | Always Active | Accelerates Force energy, increasing the **power of Force skills**. Acquired from monsters or Adventure Coin Merchant . |
| **Mind Supporter** | Always Active | Expands mental energy, increasing **maximum FP**. Acquired from Guild Raid or Accretia Contribution Shop . |
| **Mind Generator** | Always Active | Activates mental energy to aid recovery, increasing **FP recovery amount**. Acquired from Cora Contribution Shop . |

### Stat Priority

To maximize the Psypher's potential, focus on the following stats in your skill tree and gear:

1.  **Force Attack:** Primary damage stat for all magical abilities.
2.  **Force Critical Rate / Force Critical Damage:** Scales directly with Unleashed Force buff.
3.  **Skill Damage:** Increases overall ability damage.
4.  **FP Recovery / Max FP:** Ensures you can sustain long skill rotations.

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "AoE Farmer" (PvE Solo Farming)
- **Focus:** Force Cyclone, Force Attack, Cooldown Reduction.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Force Cyclone** first, then **Unleashed Force**.
- **Playstyle:** Group mobs -> Activate **Unleashed Force** -> Drop **Force Cyclone** -> Clean up with **Force Spear**.
- **Tip:** Use **Anima's Fragment** to taunt elite mobs off you while you AoE down the pack.

### 2. The "Kiting Mage" (Small-Scale PvP / Duels)
- **Focus:** Force Fist, Movement Speed, Force Critical Damage.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Force Fist**, **Force Explosion**, and **Emergency Armor** passive.
- **Playstyle:** Open with **Force Fist** to slow -> Maintain distance with **Force Spear** -> Burst with **Force Explosion** when they get close.
- **Tip:** The knockback from basic skills can interrupt enemy attacks; use it to cancel casters.

### 3. The "Siege Controller" (Large-Scale GvG)
- **Focus:** Force Cyclone, Awakening, Skill Damage.
- **Priority Skills:** Max **Force Cyclone**, **Awakening**, and **Force Skill Support**.
- **Playstyle:** Position behind your frontline tanks -> Drop **Force Cyclone** on enemy clusters -> Use **Force Explosion** to burst priority targets.
- **Tip:** Time your **Emergency Armor** passive; the invincibility proc can save you from enemy assassins.

## Verdict

The **Psypher** is arguably one of the most well-rounded classes in *RF Online Next*. It excels in PvE farming due to its powerful AoE skills like Force Cyclone, performs admirably in PvP with its slows and burst damage, and is forgiving enough for beginners thanks to the Emergency Armor invincibility passive .

Whether you are a free-to-play player looking for an efficient farming class, or a veteran seeking a strong magical DPS for endgame content, the Psypher is a fantastic choice. It scales well with investment, is consistently ranked as "Tier 1" in community tier lists, and is officially recommended by the developers as a starter class .

If you enjoy the traditional "wizard" playstyle—staying at range, controlling crowds, and unleashing magical devastation—the Psypher is your suit.
