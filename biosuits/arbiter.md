## Details

 **Info: Biosuit designed for close-range to mid-range combat.<br>Suitable for Force attacks and focused assault operations.**

 **Description:** Arbiters decide life and death on the battlefield. <br>Once one dons an Arbiter biosuit, <br>they become a whirling blaze of burning blades, <br>sweeping across the field and unleashing explosive power in offensive operations.

## Overview

Arbiters are close-quarters fighters who excel at locking down enemies and sustaining through damage. They can pull targets out of position, create zones that massively buff their own stats, and restore health based on the number of enemies hit. While they lack the raw range of Punishers, their versatility in both PvE and PvP makes them a solid "S-Tier" choice.

- **Role:** Hybrid Attacker (Burst/Sustain)
- **Weapon:** Force Blade
- **Attack Type:** Melee / Force Attack
- **Special Mechanic:** Sacred Area (Self-buffing zone)

## Recommended Builds

### 1. The "Immortal Farmer" (PvE Solo)
- **Focus:** Force Attack, Cooldown Reduction, HP Drain.
- **Playstyle:** Group mobs -> Cast **Sacred Area** -> Use **Instant Judgment** to heal to full -> Clean up with Spatial Slash.
- **Tip:** Prioritize "Endurance" passive to make your healing potions more effective during boss fights.

### 2. The "Initiator" (GvG / PvP)
- **Focus:** Force Defense Penetration, Status Resistance, Movement Speed.
- **Playstyle:** Flank the enemy -> Pull a key target with **Law of the Executor** -> Pop **Blade of Repose** to deal damage without being stunned.
- **Tip:** Coordinate with a Punisher or Dreadnought. Pull an enemy into their AoE for an instant kill.

## Verdict

The **Arbiter** is the definitive "jack-of-all-trades" for *RF Online Next*. It sacrifices the extreme range of other classes for unmatched sustain and dueling potential. Whether you are playing solo, running dungeons, or pushing ranks in PvP, the Arbiter is a safe, high-reward investment that scales well into the endgame. If you enjoy dynamic melee combat with a lifesteal mechanic, this is the class for you.
